About

When I first moved to New York, I was told that I was not a local until I had a go-to brunch place (I was also told that I didn't know how to pronounce "Houston"). Sometimes it can be difficult to get your bearings in a new place.

This website is an attempt to make getting to know Los Angeles a little easier, and hopefully more fun. The homepage displays a map with icons for different types of activities: ACTIVE, ARTS/ENTERTAINMENT, FOOD, DRINK, and OTHER. Click on an icon to learn more about it, or view all the places in List form by clicking on the link above.

Users log in and add new places to visit via the "Add a Place" button. You can acknowledge that you've visited a place and up/down vote it from the main map. The "Top Ten" link considers all the upvotes and ranks the ten most popular places to visit in Los Angeles. Make it to all ten and you're officially a local (and you'll unlock a special prize)!

Happy Exploring...
